The 5-Layer AI Skill Pyramid organizes AI expertise from foundational technical skills to strategic leadership:

*   **Layer 1 (Data & Infrastructure):** Foundational skills in data collection, cleaning, storage, and managing AI computing resources.
*   **Layer 2-3 (Models & Engineering):** Expertise in understanding and developing core AI/ML algorithms, then building, deploying, and maintaining AI systems (MLOps).
*   **Layer 4 (Product & Solution Design):** Ability to translate AI capabilities into valuable, user-centric products and business solutions, focusing on user experience and ethical implications.
*   **Layer 5 (Strategy & Leadership):** Defining the AI vision, governance, ethical frameworks, and integrating AI effectively into overall business strategy.
